Switzerland 2025

In 2025, I traveled to Switzerland with my boyfriend to visit family and friends, and it quickly became one of my favorite trips. Instead of staying in one place, we explored the country on our own, taking trains, visiting different cities, and enjoying the breathtaking mountains and lakes. One fun fact: almost everyone in Switzerland speaks English, which made communication super easy. Traveling the country by ourselves made the experience even more special and unforgettable.
